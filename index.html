Welcome to Guild Wars Evolution!

Guild Wars and Guild Wars 2 are massive multi-player online role-playing games (MMORPG; often shortened to MMO) in which a player can create characters in order to participate in quests and storylines within the online world. Guild Wars Evolution is a players tribute to the Guild Wars universe.

If you are new to Guild Wars, you can check out all of the original Guild Wars releases and what they offered both in story and game-play. If you have played Guild Wars before you will find a little of the lore linking Guild Wars and Guild Wars 2 and a breakdown of the changes Guild Wars 2 has brought. These changes are compared and contrasted with their counterparts from the original Guild Wars and supplemented by the authors opinions.

What you will not find on Guild Wars Evolution is any type of Player-versus-Player content, any in-depth analysis of game mechanics, or other technical aspects of the game. Guild Wars Evolution concentrates strictly on Player-versus-Environment, or story-line, content and attempts to support and maintain the elements of role-play and immersion. Guild Wars - History and Lore

Read through a series of recovered entries from the journal of a warrior named Athena Dominus. Athena traversed the lands of Tyria (Prophecies and Eye of the North), Cantha (Factions), and Elona (Nightfall) during perilous and exciting times. While the entire journal could not be recovered, there is a good amount of what life was like in her time.
